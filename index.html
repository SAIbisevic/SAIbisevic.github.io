Abstract

Semin Ibisevic is co-founder and Chief Technology Officer of Next Gate Tech, a fintech he established in 2019 together with Davide Martucci. He holds a Master's degree in Quantitative Finance and a Bachelor's in Econometrics and Operations Research, both from Erasmus University Rotterdam. Prior to founding Next Gate Tech, he served as Technology Lead and Quantitative Strategist at Next Gate Capital, with earlier roles at Euronext and AEGON Asset Management.
